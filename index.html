About the Laboratory

The Laboratory of Polymer Composites at K. Zhubanov Aktobe Regional University develops and studies flame-retardant polymer materials and composites based on epoxy and vinyl ester resins. Its main research directions include designing flame-retardant additives, investigating the thermal, mechanical and fire-protective properties of materials, and modifying inorganic fillers for high-performance composites.
